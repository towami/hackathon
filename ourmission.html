Our Mission

Preserve, Protect, and Celebrate Fashion Heritage

Fashion is more than just clothing—it’s a living record of culture, craftsmanship, and creativity. At MoMu and beyond, archives hold the stories of our past, yet many of these treasures remain at risk of being lost to time. Our mission is to bring these archives to life, unite a global community of fashion enthusiasts, and ensure that the legacy of fashion is preserved for generations to come.

Why We’re Here

We are here to:

  • Unite: Build a global platform for fashion museums, enthusiasts, and archives lovers to share and celebrate their collections.
  • Preserve: Digitize, protect, and document rare and historic fashion pieces to prevent their loss.
  • Inspire: Empower a new generation of creators, historians, and fashion enthusiasts to explore and honor the artistry of the past.
  • Celebrate: Champion the craftsmanship and stories that shape fashion’s legacy, connecting communities worldwide.
